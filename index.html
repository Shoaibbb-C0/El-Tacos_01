Our Story

A Taste of Mexico
in Kharghar

EL TACOS was born from a deep love of Mexican street food culture — the sizzling trompos, hand-pressed tortillas, and bold sauces that define the streets of Mexico City.

We brought that spirit to Kharghar, Navi Mumbai — a cozy kitchen where every taco is crafted fresh, every bite tells a story, and every visit feels like a mini fiesta.
